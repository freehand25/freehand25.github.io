Key Idea

When transferring embodied agents from source domain to the unseen domain, the previous methods rely on human demonstrations which are often collected through tele-operation. The collection process is time-consuming and brings high costs in money, which hinders the autonomy. Our framework named Human Demonstration-Free (HDFree) supports adapting embodied models without human demonstrations in any new environment. With only visual observations captured from cameras, which are labor-free and easy to collect, our method promotes the automation and self-reliance of embodiments. Given zero human demonstrations in the new environment, our method achieves 34% improvements on success rate, while the previous method finetuned based on 200 demonstrations only brings 17% improvements on success rate.
